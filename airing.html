00Overview

Because of the previous fire in Napa, the air quality in San Francisco was very bad. Some people may not notice that, but it hurts our bodies.

So I am thinking to design a device that can help people check the air quality and switch the window automatically.

01Features & Goals

Features:
1.Measure outdoor PM2.5 and control the opening and closing of Windows automatically.
2.User will receive a notification when the PM2.5 is over 35~75μg/m³.
3.Set a timer to close the window.

Goals:
Helps people prevent the PM2.5 from harming the health.

02use cases & use case scenarios

Use Case 1: User wants to open the window.
Use Case Scenarios:
-Press the button.(white button)

Use Case 2: User wants to switch to automatic mode.
Use Case Scenarios:
-Double press the button.(white button)
-Window will close automatically when the PM2.5 is over 200 μg/m³ and receive a message when the PM2.5 is over 300 μg/m³.

Use Case 3: User wants to set a timer to close window.
Use Case Scenarios:
-Press the timer button under manual mode.(black button)

11Conclusion

This project has actually helped me a lot. I got pneumothorax several years ago, so I pay special attention to air quality. Recent wildfires in California have made San Francisco’s air quality once among the worst in the world. I also hope that more people can pay attention to the influence of air quality on our physical health through this project.
