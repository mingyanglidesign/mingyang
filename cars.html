00Overview

What’s important in any creative act is the process of self-expression. Creative experiences can help children express and cope with their feelings. Creativity also fosters mental growth in children by providing opportunities for trying out new ideas, and new ways of thinking and problem-solving.

The purpose of this project is to cultivate children’s creativity and let them design their own toy cars. Children can choose the shape, color, sound and stickers of their toy cars. We also provide a game to improve children’s reaction ability.

01Research

What’s important in any creative act is the process of self-expression. Creative experiences can help children express and cope with their feelings. Creativity also fosters mental growth in children by providing opportunities for trying out new ideas, and new ways of thinking and problem-solving.

Every child is born with an imagination. At times, parents and adults nurture children’s imaginations and take joy in their creative thoughts and acts. Other times, we might (deliberately or unknowingly) stifle children’s imaginations, perhaps concerned that children don’t understand what’s real. Allowing children to use their imagination is helpful and there are many benefits of pretend play in child development.
