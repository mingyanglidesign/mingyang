00Overview

When you take picture of a person, there is usually a purpose behind your actions. It could be for a celebration, for art, for fun, or to communicate something more. With environmental portraits, the photographer is capturing the image of a person and showing the audience what this person is made of. Personality, occupation, social life, and other details can be portrayed in an environmental portrait.
